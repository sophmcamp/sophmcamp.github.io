About

I was born in Washington, DC and raised in Gaithersburg, MD. I also lived in Alexandria, VA for several years. So, one could say I've been all over the DMV.

I'm enthusiastic about creativity and innovation in general and hope to fulfill this passion in my work. Check out my resume to learn more about my experiences and skills.

In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, going on walks, reading, playing the guitar, skateboarding, and exploring the world.
