About

Hello! My name is Sophia, and I'm a rising senior at Virginia Tech with a major in Professional and Technical Writing and a minor in Human-Computer Interaction.

I was born in Washington, DC, raised in Gaithersburg, MD, and have also lived in Alexandria, VA for several years. So, one could say I've been all over the DMV.

I aspire to contribute to concise, accessible, and user-friendly documentation, reports, or products in the software industry. In addition, I aspire to spread awareness about issues in cyberspace. In general, I'm enthusiastic about fostering an atmosphere in which creativity and innovation can thrive.

In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, attending concerts, reading, doing yoga, playing the guitar, ukulele, and keyboard, hiking, running, and traveling.
