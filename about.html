About

Hello! My name is Sophia, and I'm a junior at Virginia Tech majoring in Professional and Technical Writing and minoring in Human-Computer Interaction. I was born in Washington, DC and was raised in Gaithersburg, MD. I have also lived in Alexandria, VA for several years. So, one could say I've been all over the DMV.

I'm passionate about the intersection of humanities and STEM and hope that this website can host a variety of my projects and interests. In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, going on walks, playing the guitar, skateboarding, and exploring the world.
