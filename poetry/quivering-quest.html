Quivering Quest

What if I’ve been in denial? I’ve been

hastening through sets of possibilities,

mingling among the ripples of seclusion,

letting myself tear apart the scenes,

shutting down when my heart speaks.

I keep having these revelations that I

push aside, acting as if my confessions

were baseless, wanting to be different

than what I’m terrified of, but alas, it’s

who I am. I can’t remain ashamed of it.


December 31, 2021
