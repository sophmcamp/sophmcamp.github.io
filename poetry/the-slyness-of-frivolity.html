The Slyness of Frivolity

It’s a mystery, jumping from one end of the spectrum

to the other, lunging into an incentivized ransom

to pardon those who may be at fault simply because

there are too many to catch and tame. I thought

that breaking free from the menacing artifices of one

would help save me from others, but I was wrong.

Going into hiding is a way to approach the situation,

but doing so will isolate oneself from incensation.

Instead, one can only expect to change injustices

by first thoroughly experiencing the world as it is.


February 13, 2021
