Only a Perception

Cowardly, intoxicating choices fail
to propel my wishes to testimony.
Integration doesn’t imply the
fulfillment that derives from
substantial, sincere investment.

Action may mean dedication,
but you’ll never find a way
to unravel my scarred soul,
which connects each strand
of memory into unbreakable
bonds of agonizing wisdom.

July 3, 2021
