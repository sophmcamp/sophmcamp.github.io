On Time

I scurry to the train, hurrying to catch the

method of transportation that is punctual.

The Swiss are into clocks and precision,

while I’m a girl who can’t keep track at all.

When I’m asked about my religious beliefs,

my remarks ought to include that my best

description consists of a gleamed, crested

question mark that is subtly Romanesque.


April 10, 2022
