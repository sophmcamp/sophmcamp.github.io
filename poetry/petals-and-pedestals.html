Petals and Pedestals

A craze, you say? How did you know that I’m

in a daze? I’m completely astray, and you,

for some reason, could sense that I’m trying

to contain the pain that arises even during

the light of day. I shouldn’t focus on this,

though, and I’ll tell you why: I’m a lotus

flower—so are you—and I see us reaching

out of the water. Even if we must encounter

more ruckus, we’ll still bloom, trust me. We’ll

earnestly endure and conquer whatever irks us

by turning towards the sun, not letting burdens

define us, that’s for sure. So, join me, if you

don’t mind; together, we can leave paranoia

behind and remind ourselves that loyalty

is not a ploy, but rather a rendering of joy.


October 6, 2020
