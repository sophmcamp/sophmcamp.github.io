Inside Out

Focusing on one task at hand gets

underneath my skin and into my blood

and becomes so consuming that I let

it control me, but I’ve had enough of

these tough games. The ultimate test

has yet to come; I can't think too much

about it because I’m scared of regret

and the unhinging liability that comes

from not explaining my ongoing quest.


November 5, 2020
