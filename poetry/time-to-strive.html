Time to Strive

Bringing awareness into perspective,

I’m showcasing what I can pursue.

I don’t usually know the intention

behind my quirks and subtleties,

but I do understand my long-term

goals and wishes that fuel my days.

So I’m living in a way that doesn’t

make sense to me often, yet I find

comfort and satisfaction overall.

I can’t exactly explain why I act

with the kindness people frequently

say I have, but I can say that

I’m only following what my heart

truly desires. I might get distracted

and tested along the way, but I’ll

always strive for justice and freedom.

I will never stop chasing love either.

I really hope I find love someday.


September 28, 2021
