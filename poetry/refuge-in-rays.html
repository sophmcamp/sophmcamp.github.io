Refuge in Rays

I’ve got no choice but to tell you this:

I’m a bit uncertain about my stance.

Horrors trace through my pulse.

I shiver as I launch myself onto the stage.

Bright lights blind me like they did when I was a kid,

a kid that lived in the comfort of light.

Back then, I thought that nothing could steal that,

yet some people have extensively tried to.

I’ve told you this so far, but should I give you the details?

I could go on and on about the nights that struck me,

scarred me, halted my jubilance, but why should I?

How can I make my life not about those nights?

That might be difficult, but I could at least

focus on the light of these days,

the light that has reappeared slowly.

It sometimes flickers on and off,

but it exists and has power,

power to guide me through

other terrors that I encounter

and the power to intuit

when something isn’t right

and when something is.


October 29, 2021
